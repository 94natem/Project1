Dinner & A Movie

Welcome to Dinner & A Movie App!

“Dinner and a Movie” is designed to provide you with a quick and painless way to search for movie showtimes and nearby restaurants all in two short steps! Start by inputting your preferred zip code and the date you would like to see your movie. “Dinner and a Movie” will generate movies currently showing in nearby theaters along with their showtimes. Once you decide on a movie, the app will recommend nearby restaurants listing their typical cuisine, address, and rating. Once you’ve chosen your restaurant, you’re all set! With those two simple steps you can plan a fun night out and not have to settle for overpriced concession snacks.
